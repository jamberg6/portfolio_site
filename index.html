About Me

I am currently a senior at Virginia Tech majoring in Computer Engineering with a focus on Software Systems and a minor in Computer Science. I enjoy creating things and solving problems.

Personal Interests

  • Food: I spend every Sunday cooking meals and snacks for the week. I also host dinner parties with my friends where we will all chip in and buy a bunch of ingredients to cook and share a meal together. I am always excited to try new types of food and restaruants wherever I go.
  • Anime: In high school my friends introduced me to Naruto, once I started the grind, I was hooked. I've watched many different genres but some of my favorite anime are Hunter x Hunter, Vinland Saga, Kaguya Sama: Love is War, Jujutsu Kaisen, Ao Ashi, and Spy x Family.
  • Video Games: Video games have always been a hobby of mine since I was little. I like all sorts of games including FPS games, sandbox games, RPG games, fighting games and more. I tend to only play one game at a time but some of my all time favorites are Team Fortress 2, Minecraft, Stardew Valley, and Valorant.
  • Soccer: I've played soccer since I was little and have enjoyed my travel league, my high school league, futsal leagues, indoor turf leagues, and summer leagues. Currently I play in intramural leagues when my school has it and the occaisonal pick up games.
  • Hiking: I was in the Boy Scouts and my family loves the outdoors so I have been on hikes in all sorts of cool places when my family travels and local trails with my friends and family. I often hike to peaks to watch the sunrise with my friends or to go swim at a waterhole at the end of a trail.
  • Lifting: Since I've been at college I have been a regular at the gym on campus as long as my studies allow me enough time. I appreciate a good pump and my favorite day is leg day.
